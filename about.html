Our Story

The Daily Grind began as a small neighborhood spot where conversation, craft, and coffee intersect. We source thoughtfully, roast with care, and cultivate a space where friends—old and new—feel at home.

From our earliest days, the aim has been simple: serve excellent coffee, nurture community, and create a welcoming, enduring ritual for your day.

Founded by a team of passionate baristas, artisans, and neighbors, we believe great coffee should be approachable, sustainable, and joyfully shared.
